Humboldt Redwoods State Park

These pictures were taken on March of 2021, a year after the Covid-19 pandemic started. After being locked up at home for more than a year, my friends and I decided to take a trip to nature, away from civilization so we could stay as safe as possible. This image captures a moment of peacefulness among friends. It reminds me of the sense of relaxation, and contentment that I felt while being in good company and in nature.
