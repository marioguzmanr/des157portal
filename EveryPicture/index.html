Every Picture Studio

Homies in the Wild

This album is a collection of snapshots that capture some of the best moments I've shared with my closest friends. From wild nights out to lazy days lounging on the couch, these pictures tell the story of our high school/college years and all the adventures we embarked on together. Whether we were exploring new places or just hanging out in familiar spots, we always made sure to document our memories with a quick snap of the camera.
